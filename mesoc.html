MESOC 2022

The 10th Biennial Middle East Special Operations Commanders Conference

  • DAYS
  • HOURS
  • MINUTES
  • SECONDS

31 October - 3 November

The SOFEX conference and exhibition are a great occasion to spark motivation and inspire Heads of State, Defence Ministers, Chiefs of Armed Forces, Air and Navy Forces, Special Operations Commanders, Counter Terrorism and Homeland Security decision makers and experts, including officials from various security agencies to expand capacity in order to address current and future challenges, place strategies in a global context and position forces for high performance.

Gathering this impressive group under one roof, the conference sets the stage for mutual cooperation with the objective of building bridges of understanding and knowledge-sharing to stay ahead of future threats to maintain global peace and security.
